PINE CITY MALL

Pine City Mall is the ultimate fashion, entertainment and dining destination in Durban. With over 30stores, 7 eateries and a host of exciting entertainment, visitors are spoilt for choice!

Prepare your senses for a world class experience at one of the largest shopping centres in the Southern Hemisphere.
